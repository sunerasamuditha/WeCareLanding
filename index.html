Cardiology

Our cardiology department provides comprehensive heart care with state-of-the-art diagnostic equipment and expert cardiologists

We offer complete cardiovascular services including diagnostic testing, interventional procedures, and cardiac surgery. Our team of experienced cardiologists and cardiac surgeons use the latest technology to treat heart conditions ranging from routine check-ups to complex cardiac procedures, ensuring the best possible outcomes for our patients.

Neurology

Our neurology department specializes in diagnosing and treating disorders of the nervous system with advanced neurological care

We provide comprehensive neurological services including treatment for stroke, epilepsy, Parkinson's disease, and other neurological conditions. Our neurologists use cutting-edge diagnostic tools and treatment methods to provide the highest quality care for patients with neurological disorders.

Hepatology

Specialized care for liver diseases with expert hepatologists and comprehensive treatment options for optimal liver health

Our hepatology department provides advanced care for all liver conditions including hepatitis, cirrhosis, and liver cancer. We offer state-of-the-art diagnostic services, medical management, and work closely with our transplant team to provide comprehensive liver care for all our patients.

Pediatrics

Complete healthcare services for children from infancy through adolescence with specialized pediatric care

Our pediatric department provides comprehensive medical care for infants, children, and adolescents. We offer preventive care, routine check-ups, immunizations, and treatment for childhood illnesses in a child-friendly environment designed to make young patients feel comfortable and safe.

Eye Care

Comprehensive eye care services with advanced technology for vision protection and eye health maintenance

Our ophthalmology department offers complete eye care services including routine eye exams, cataract surgery, retinal treatments, and LASIK procedures. We use the latest diagnostic equipment and surgical techniques to preserve and improve your vision health.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our healthcare services, appointments, and medical procedures

How do I schedule an appointment with a doctor?

You can schedule an appointment by calling our main number, using our online appointment system on this website, or visiting our reception desk in person. We offer flexible scheduling to accommodate your needs.

What insurance plans do you accept?

We accept most major insurance plans including Medicare, Medicaid, and private insurance providers. Please contact our billing department to verify coverage for your specific plan and understand your benefits.

What should I bring to my first appointment?

Please bring a valid photo ID, your insurance card, a list of current medications, any relevant medical records from previous providers, and your method of payment for any copays or deductibles.

Do you offer emergency services 24/7?

Yes, our emergency department is open 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. We have emergency physicians and nurses on staff at all times to handle urgent medical situations and provide immediate care.

How can I access my medical records?

You can access your medical records through our patient portal online, by calling our medical records department, or by visiting in person with proper identification. We prioritize the security and privacy of your health information.

What specialty services do you offer?

We offer a wide range of specialty services including cardiology, neurology, orthopedics, oncology, pediatrics, women's health, and many other specialized medical fields with board-certified physicians.
